# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

The full text is available at:
https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/

## Reporting

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers.
All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards
of acceptable behavior and will take appropriate and fair corrective action
in response to any behavior that they deem inappropriate.
